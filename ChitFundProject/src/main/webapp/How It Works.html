The Chit Process - How It Works

The chit fund begins on a specified date, and the members contribute a fixed monthly installment to the fund. Each month, a reverse auction is conducted where the members bid for the prize money.

The winner is the one who offers the lowest discount, and they receive the prize money for that month. This process repeats each month, allowing every member to eventually win the prize money.

The auction process ensures transparency, where all members can participate and bid for the pot. It creates a savings habit among members while providing access to lump sum amounts in times of need.
