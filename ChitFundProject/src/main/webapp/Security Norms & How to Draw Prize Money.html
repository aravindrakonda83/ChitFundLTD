Security Norms & How to Draw Prize Money

To ensure transparency and security, chit funds have set security norms that protect both the foreman and the members. The norms include periodic audits, proper documentation, and strict adherence to financial regulations.

To draw prize money, a member must submit a request and fulfill all the necessary formalities before the auction. Once the member wins the auction, they are entitled to withdraw the prize money after the commission deduction.

The process is designed to be transparent and secure. Each member's identity and contributions are properly documented. There are also legal safeguards in place to ensure that the prize money is distributed fairly among all participants.
