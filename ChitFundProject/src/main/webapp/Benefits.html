Benefits of Chits

Chit funds offer numerous benefits to their members. These include the ability to save regularly, access lump sums when needed, and earn a dividend on the monthly contributions.

Unlike traditional savings accounts or investment schemes, chit funds allow flexibility and are backed by the collective effort of the group.

Moreover, chit funds help build a financial support system within a group of people. Members can also benefit from lower interest rates on loans compared to other financial options. This fosters a community spirit where members support each other in achieving financial goals.
